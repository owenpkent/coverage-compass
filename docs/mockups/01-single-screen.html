Here is what your letter says

Renewal request
June 17, 2026 32 days from today

What this means

Colorado Medicaid wants to check that you still qualify for coverage. You need to send your renewal by June 17, 2026.

If you do nothing: your Medicaid will end on July 1, 2026.

What to do next

  1. 1 Renew online at PEAK Fastest. Sign in at peak.colorado.gov.
  2. 2 Call Health First Colorado 1-800-221-3943, Monday to Friday, 7:30am to 5:15pm.
  3. 3 Call CCDC if you need help 303-839-1775. They are free and on your side.
