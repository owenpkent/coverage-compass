What this letter says, in plain words

Colorado Medicaid wants to check that you still qualify for coverage. You renew once a year. The state needs your renewal form by June 17, 2026.

If you do nothing: your Medicaid will end on July 1, 2026, even if you still qualify. Most people who lose coverage at renewal lose it for paperwork reasons, not because they no longer qualify.

What to do next

  1. Renew online at PEAK peak.colorado.gov. Fastest way. Takes about 20 minutes.
  2. Or call Health First Colorado 1-800-221-3943, Monday to Friday, 7:30am to 5:15pm.
  3. Need help? Call CCDC 303-839-1775. CCDC is free and on your side.

What to have ready

  • Your case number from the letter (top right).
  • Most recent pay stubs, if you work.
  • Your Social Security award letter, if you get SSDI or SSI.
  • HCBS waiver paperwork, if you have a waiver.
